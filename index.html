Hi there! My name is Mike and welcome to my learning journal.

I started my coding learning journey in December 2023. I began with YouTube tutorials from SuperSimpleDev

The first course I started with was his HTML & CSS Full Course - Beginner to Pro

From there, I advanced to his JavaScript Full Course - Beginner to Pro

Introduction to Scrimba

After I completed both of SuperSimpleDev's great tutorials, I was a bit unsure of where to proceed. That's when I stumbled upon Scrimba and their Frontend Developer Career Path.

I fell in love with Scrimba's interactive learning experience, and as of January 2024, I am about halfway done with the career path.
